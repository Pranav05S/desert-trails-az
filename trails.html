Arizona Trail Guide

Find your perfect hike in the desert Southwest

Whether you are a first-time hiker or a seasoned trailblazer, Desert Trails AZ has a trail for you. Below you will find detailed information on some of the best hikes in and around the Phoenix and Tempe area. Use the comparison table to quickly find a trail that matches your skill level and schedule.

Featured Trails

Camelback Mountain

Difficulty: Hard

Distance: 5.5 miles

Location: Phoenix, AZ

One of the most iconic hikes in Arizona. The summit offers panoramic views of the entire Phoenix valley. Bring plenty of water and start early to avoid the heat. Best tackled in cooler months between October and April.

South Mountain Park

Difficulty: Easy

Distance: 2.5 miles

Location: Phoenix, AZ

A great beginner-friendly option close to Tempe. Wide, well-marked trails with beautiful desert scenery and ancient petroglyphs along the way. Perfect for families and those new to desert hiking.

Fremont Saddle

Difficulty: Moderate

Distance: 5.8 miles

Location: Superstition Mountains, AZ

A classic Arizona hike that rewards you with stunning views of Weaver's Needle and the surrounding Superstition Wilderness. Drive the scenic Apache Trail to reach the trailhead.
